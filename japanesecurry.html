Japanese Style Curry

Description

This delicious, savory curry is a fan favorite at my house, and I'm sure it will be at your's too!

Ingredients

  • Yellow Onions: 2
  • Russet Potatos: 1-2
  • Whole Carrots: 2
  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs: 1 & 1/2 lb
  • Kosher Salt: 1/4 tsp
  • Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  • Ginger: 1 knob
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Fuji Apple
  • Neutral Oil
  • Chicken Broth/Stock: 4 Cups
  • Honey: 1 Tbsp
  • Soy Sauce: 1 Tbsp
  • Ketchup: 1 Tbsp
  • Japanese Curry Roux: 1 Package
  • Tonkatsu Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the onions into thick wedges.
  2. Peel and chop the carrots into rolling wedges.
  3. Peel the potatoes and the cut them into about 1" pieces. Soak in water for 15 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. Grate the ginger until you have around 1 tsp. Mince garlic and set the two aside for later.
  5. Peel the apple and set aside for later.
  6. Trim your chicken and then cut into bit-size pieces. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat and then add in the onion.
  8. Saute the onions until translucent and tender: about 5 minutes. Do not stir too often so as to allow to become golden.
  9. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger and mix well.
  10. Add the chicken and cook, stirring often, until it is no longer pink on the outside.
  11. Add the chicken stock.
  12. Add the carrots and simmer for 20 minutes.
  13. Add the soy sauce, ketchup, apple, honey, and any other additional condiments.
  14. Use a fine mesh strainer to skim the scum from the surface of the broth.
  15. Add the potatoes and allow to cook until tender: about 10 minutes.
  16. Cut the heat and add the curry roux 1-2 blocks at time by allowing it to dissolve in a ladle and then mixing into the broth. Repeat with all of the blocks.
  17. Simmer on medium-low heat, until thickened.
  18. Season to taste with salt.
  19. Serve over rice or noodles and enjoy!
