Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These delicous, gooey brown butter chocolate chip cookies will rock your world!

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter: 2 Sticks
  • White Sugar: 1/2 Cup
  • Brown Sugar: 1 & 1/2 Cups
  • Eggs: 2
  • All Purpose Flour: 11 & 1/2 oz
  • Kosher Salt: 1 Tbsp
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 tsp
  • Cinnamon: 3/4 tsp
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Semi-sweet Chocolate Bars
  • Flaky Sea Salt
  • Nuts (Optional)

Steps

  1. Brown 2 sticks of unsalted butter. After browning, place in a measuring cup and refrigerate for 1 hour or until solidified.
  2. In the bowl of stand mixer, add the butter and your white and brown sugar. Mix well to combine.
  3. Next add 2 cold eggs to the mixture and combine.
  4. In a separate medium bowl, whisk together your flour, kosher salt, baking soda, and cinnamon.
  5. Add half of the dry ingredients to the stand mixer, mix to combine, and then add the other half.
  6. Add the chocolate chips/chopped up chocolate bars, stir until just combined and then remove dough from mixer. Place in a bowl and refrigerate for at least an hour or up to 2 days.
  7. Optional: Toast nuts and add to dough.
  8. Roll the cookie dough into balls and then break in half and set on a cookie sheet. Bake in a 350 degrees oven for 12-17 minutes or until they have reached your preferred doneness. Top with sea salt.
  9. Let cool for a few minutes, serve and enjoy.
